🧸 LKG & UKG Program

🌈 Play-Based Learning | Skill Development | Happy Learning

Our LKG & UKG program is designed to create a joyful and engaging learning environment where children develop essential early skills through play, creativity, and structured activities. We focus on building a strong foundation in language, numbers, and social behavior while nurturing curiosity and confidence.

✔️ Play-Based Learning
✔️ Activity-Focused
✔️ Personal Care

📚 Subjects & Activities

  • Alphabet Recognition (A-Z)
  • Phonics & Basic Reading
  • Numbers (1–100)
  • Shapes & Colors
  • Rhymes & Storytelling
  • Drawing & Coloring

🧠 Key Learning Areas

  • Language development
  • Basic math skills
  • Motor skills development
  • Listening & speaking skills
  • Creativity & imagination
  • Social interaction skills

🎨 Activities & Fun Learning

  • Art & craft activities
  • Music & dance sessions
  • Story-based learning
  • Puzzle & brain games
  • Indoor & outdoor play

📈 Development Focus

  • Confidence building
  • Communication skills
  • Behavior & discipline
  • Independent learning habits
  • Emotional development

👨‍👩‍👧 Who Should Join?

  • Kids aged 3 to 5 years
  • First-time learners
  • Children needing early skill development

🏆 Program Outcomes

  • Strong early foundation
  • Improved speaking & listening
  • Basic reading & writing readiness
  • Confidence & social skills
