Women Day Celebration at Shiv Shiksha Niketan Sr Sec School Pehowa

Shiv Shiksha Niketan Sr Sec School celebrated International Women’s Day with great enthusiasm and respect. The campus was filled with a spirit of empowerment, equality, and appreciation for the contributions of women in society.

The celebration began with a special assembly, where inspiring speeches were delivered to highlight the importance of women’s rights and gender equality. Tributes were paid to influential women who have made remarkable contributions in various fields.

Students showcased vibrant cultural performances including songs, dances, and skits, emphasizing the strength, resilience, and achievements of women. The program beautifully reflected the message of respect, empowerment, and equal opportunities for all..
