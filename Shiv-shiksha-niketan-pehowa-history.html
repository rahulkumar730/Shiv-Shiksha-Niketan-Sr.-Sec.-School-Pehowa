History

Home | History

Shiv Shiksha Niketan Sr. Sec. School, Pehowa, was established with the noble aim of spreading quality education in the region and creating a strong foundation for young learners. Since its inception, the school has been dedicated to nurturing students with academic excellence, discipline, and moral values.

Starting with a small number of students, the institution has steadily grown over the years into a well-recognized educational center in Pehowa. With continuous efforts and commitment from management and faculty, the school expanded its infrastructure, academic programs, and co-curricular activities to meet the evolving needs of modern education.

Throughout its journey, Shiv Shiksha Niketan has maintained its core values of integrity, dedication, and holistic development. Today, it stands as a symbol of trust and quality education, shaping generations of students who contribute positively to society.

The school continues to move forward with the same vision, striving to achieve new milestones while preserving its rich educational legacy.
