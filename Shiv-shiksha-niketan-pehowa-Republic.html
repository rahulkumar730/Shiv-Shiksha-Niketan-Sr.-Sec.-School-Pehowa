Republic Day Celebration - 26 January

Republic Day is celebrated on 26th January every year to honor the day when the Constitution of India came into effect in 1950.

The celebration includes flag hoisting, parades, and cultural events that showcase India's rich heritage and unity.

Students and staff actively participate in patriotic performances, speeches, and activities reflecting national pride.
